Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

We believe privacy is a practice, not a promise. This policy explains what information Stillpoint collects, why, and how you can control it.

Who we are

Stillpoint is a wellness product built by Tai, operating as Aya Healer. You can reach us at support@stillpoint.day for any questions about this policy or your data.

What we collect right now

At this stage, Stillpoint is pre-launch. The only information we collect is the email address you submit through our waitlist form at stillpoint.day.

We use it for one purpose: to send you one email when the Stillpoint app becomes available for download. We may also send infrequent product updates related to the launch.

We do not:

  • Sell your email to anyone, ever.
  • Share it with advertisers or data brokers.
  • Use it to train AI models.
  • Add you to unrelated newsletters or marketing funnels.

How we store it

Your email is stored securely with our email service provider, Buttondown, who processes data on our behalf. They are subject to their own privacy standards. You can read their privacy policy at buttondown.com/legal/privacy.

Cookies and tracking

stillpoint.day uses Plausible Analytics to understand aggregate site traffic. This analytics provider does not use cookies, does not track you across websites, and does not collect personal information. No tracking pixels, no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel.

Your rights

You can:

  • Unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of any email we send.
  • Request deletion of your data by emailing support@stillpoint.day. We'll confirm and delete within 30 days.
  • Request a copy of any data we hold on you, at the same address.

If you are in the European Union, the United Kingdom, or California, you have additional rights under GDPR and CCPA respectively. Email us and we will honor them.

When Stillpoint launches

Once the app is available, we will update this policy to cover additional data — such as stillness session data, app usage, and optional HealthKit integration. You will be notified by email before any new data collection begins.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page and notify existing subscribers by email.

Contact

Questions, concerns, or requests — write to us at support@stillpoint.day. We read every email personally.
