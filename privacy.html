Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Stillpoint is a quiet app. That extends to how we handle your data — we collect as little as possible, keep it on your device, and never sell or share it.

Who we are

Stillpoint is built by Tai, operating as Aya Healer. You can reach us at support@stillpoint.day with any questions about this policy or your data.

The app — what Stillpoint collects

Stillpoint stores your practice data — sitting sessions, journal entries, visitor choices, and settings — locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable iCloud sync (see below).

We do not collect analytics, crash reports, usage metrics, advertising identifiers, or any personal information from inside the app. There are no third-party SDKs in Stillpoint. No data is sent to our servers.

iCloud sync (optional)

If you are signed into iCloud, Stillpoint can sync your practice archive across your devices using Apple's CloudKit private database. Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account — not ours. Apple encrypts it in transit and at rest. We have no access to it. You can disable iCloud sync at any time in iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud.

Apple Health (optional)

If you grant permission, Stillpoint writes Mindful Session records to Apple Health for each completed sitting. We write only — we never read from Health. You can revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Stillpoint.

Dedication line

The optional dedication you set in Settings ("I sit for ___") is stored locally in your device's preferences. It is never transmitted, never tracked, never logged. It is private.

Screen Time / Family Controls (optional)

If you use Retreat Mode, Stillpoint requests access to Apple's Screen Time framework to place voluntary app shields. The list of apps you choose to shield is stored on-device only and is never transmitted to us.

The website — stillpoint.day

The only personal information we collect through this website is the email address you voluntarily submit on the waitlist form. We use it to notify you when the app is available and to send infrequent product updates. We do not sell it, share it with advertisers, or use it to train AI models.

Your email is stored with our provider, Buttondown. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send, or by writing to support@stillpoint.day.

stillpoint.day uses Plausible Analytics for aggregate site traffic. Plausible uses no cookies and collects no personal information. There are no tracking pixels, no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel.

Your rights

You can:

  • Delete all app data by deleting the app from your device. iCloud data can be removed in iCloud settings.
  • Request deletion of your email by writing to support@stillpoint.day. We will confirm and delete within 30 days.
  • Request a copy of any data we hold, at the same address.

If you are in the European Union, the United Kingdom, or California, you have additional rights under GDPR and CCPA respectively. Email us and we will honor them.

Children

Stillpoint is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be announced by email to waitlist subscribers.

Contact

Questions, requests, or anything else — write to support@stillpoint.day. We read every message personally.
